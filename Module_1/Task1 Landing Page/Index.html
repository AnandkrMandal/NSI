    Frequently Asked Questions

    Web3 refers to the third era of the internet, characterized by decentralized protocols, blockchain technology, and user empowerment. It aims to create a more open, secure, and user-centric internet.
    Web2 relies on centralized services controlled by a few entities, while Web3 promotes decentralization, giving users more control over their data and interactions.
    Explore blockchain development, learn about decentralized technologies, and engage with Web3 communities. Stay informed about emerging projects and technologies in the space.
    While cryptocurrencies play a significant role, Web3 encompasses a broader vision of a decentralized internet with enhanced privacy, security, and user control.
    Web3 aims to enhance privacy by reducing reliance on centralized servers that collect and control user data. Users have more control over their personal information..
